To explore is to be human

Paddle the Expanse

I started this project in 2026 so that I can share my love and passion of our natural world. My hope is that I can inspire others to adventure and explore the world around them. What follows are snapshots of my own adventures as well as a showcase of the local regional parks around the province of Saskatchewan and throughout Canada.

The South Saskatchewan River will always hold a special place in my heart as it has been the gateway to so many adventures in my life. I've been inspired by many explorers in our province and throughout Canada. Canada is a vast expanse and I hope you will join me in adventuring and exploring this beautiful land.
